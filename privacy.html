Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

BendLogic is built as a tool for professional electricians. We value your privacy and aim to be transparent about how the app works.

1. Data Collection

BendLogic is a utility tool. We do not collect, store, or share any personal identification information (PII). All calculations and visualizations are performed locally on your device.

2. Permissions

The app may require certain device permissions to function correctly (such as storage for saving presets), but no data from your device is transmitted to our servers or third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Play Services for app distribution and updates. Please refer to Google’s privacy policy regarding how they handle data for Android applications.
